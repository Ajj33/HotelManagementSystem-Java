Hotel Management System (HMS) - Project scope:

* Requires staff to enter quantity of meals.
* Various beverages can be added to the purchase.
* Customer's room number can be selected, specifically for room delivery.
* Has the option to add delivery fees to the total.
* Has the option to include tax fees to the total. 
* Indicates Total cost of - meal, drink and delivery sepratley. 
* SubTotal indicates the total without the additional fees.
* Total indicates the sum of everything including the additional fees.
* Indicates local time  and date.
* Able to convert pounds in five different currencies. 
* Able to reset entered data.
* Has an inbuilt calculator.
* Able to print receipt of total purchase including other fees.

Able to Exit the HMS
